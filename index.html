About us

Kamvas Cattery is a Cat adoption and rescue center. As an animal welfare organisation we acknowledge all animals as sentient beings and strive to prevent their cruelty and exploitation. We aim to achieve this by proactively promoting animal welfare, rescuing stray cats and getting them warm homes through adoption. Our mission is to maintain a high profile within the community through visibility and interaction.
